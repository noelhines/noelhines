MASS COMMUNITCATIONS MAJOR 

 

Favorite Brand 

 

 When looking through all my favorite brands my mind went everywhere, I was thinking all big names like Nike and Lululemon but I ultimately landed on Pleasing. Pleasing a beauty company founded by singer and actor, Harry Styles, designed ‘to bring joyful experiences that excite and blue the boundaries.” Aside from the brand being attached to Harry Styles, they have a great line of products. They sell mostly nail polish and skincare, but also have a few clothing items and some makeup.

 Why Pleasing?

Their brand motto is ‘we’re Pleasing, never perfect” They prioritize people and the planet by giving back to communities and causes they believe in such as Queercircle, Cool Earth, and Nest. On top of being a brand I thoroughly enjoy; they have a great aesthetic to their website. With each of their different micro-collections, each featuring new products, comes a new aesthetic to fit the theme. 

My Inspiration

 My personal favorite collection is Shroom Bloom, and its where I am drawing a lot of my inspiration from. First glance at the website it seems to be clean and has a simple interface. The home page features the newest collection with large and bright graphics that cover the page and a solid banner that always stays at the top. It looks like they used a custom front on their website but a very similar one is Romana Demi. They are sans serif fonts that have their own personality in each space you see them. The header is more of a bold font with a heavier weight and all the text throughout the webpage is clean and easy to read. The website overall uses a great color scheme, and it has a great user interface. They use large clear photos of their products and the colors on the page correlate with the products in each collection making for a seamless webpage. I plan to find some similar fonts used on the Pleasing website as they have a custom one and draw some inspiration from their color schemes and design layout. Pleasing uses the same font at different weights throughout their page which makes for a more uniform look in my opinion, I will do something similar on my page. For my header I plan to keep it simple but still have some color to it. My colors now are somewhat diffused Pink, Purple, green, and marigold. The header won’t have much happening though just simply what the website is.
